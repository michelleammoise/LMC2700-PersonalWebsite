LMC 2700 Assignment 5: Ramblin' Romance

Ramblin’ Romance is a Georgia Tech-themed dating simulation game or a visual novel in which the player is on a mission to land a date to a party Friday night. The player is allowed to input their name and select which major they want to be, which uniquely assigns the player statistical values for intellect, charisma (a.k.a “rizz”), and attractiveness. The three potential love interests (male: Chad, female: Olivia, and non-binary: Jordan) are assigned their own statistics in which the player wants to accumulate a high enough skill level to be deemed as “compatible” with their interest so that they can receive the “good” ending. The game, narrated by Buzz, spans a four-day time span where each day the players are given important choices that heavily impact the player’s potential to receive the “good”, “neutral” or “bad” endings.
